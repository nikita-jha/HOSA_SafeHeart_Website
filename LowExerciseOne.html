SafeHeart: Low Risk Exercise

Exercise Plan for Low Risk Patients

    For people whose blood levels are only slightly deviated from normal biomarker levels, we suggest that they focus on building a strong cardiovascular system by exercising 30 to 60 minutes a day. These exercises consist of anything that raise the patients heart beat and promotes good blood pressure.

    Suggestions for this include:

  • Running
  • Swimming
  • Brisk Walking
  • Mountain Climbers
  • High Knees
  • Jumping Jacks
