SafeHeart: Diet Plan

Plan your diet to remain healthy!

Having a heart-healthy diet is vital for the well being of any patient. Although there is not necessarily any calorie intake limit, the patient should consume the correct type of food in correct proportions in order to maintain a stable diet. This means that if needed, the patient can consume large amounts of calories as long as these calories come from healthy sources. Some heart healthy foods that the patient can consume include:

  • Blueberries
  • Salmon
  • Spinach
  • Almonds
  • Tomatoes
  • Avocados
  • Asparagus

    • There are also many tips that we recommend when it comes to choosing the correct foods to help reduce the risk of the heart disease escalating:

  • Eat a variety of oils ranging olive oil, canola, peanuts as well as foods containing natural fats (nuts, seeds, avocado, olives, soy, fish)
  • Avoid fried and processed foods
  • Switch to low-fat or non-fat dairy products
  • Include legumes such as baked beans, soybeans, lentils, and tofu
  • Trim fat from meat
  • Avoid high sodium content
